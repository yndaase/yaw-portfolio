About Me

A brief look into my background and what drives my work in technology.

I am Yaw Ndaase Mensuoh, a passionate systems architect and technologist from Ghana. My mission is to build robust, scalable platforms that create meaningful impact, particularly in the education sector.

As the founder of Vision Education, I have spearheaded the development of Ghana's premier WASSCE preparation platform, integrating AI to provide real-time, examiner-level feedback to students. I thrive on solving complex technical challenges, designing elegant architectures, and leading projects from conception to global deployment.

Whether it's deploying secure Firebase rules, crafting beautiful UI designs, or architecting AI engines, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the web.
